About Us

Who Are We?

We are the Artificial Intelligence Student Chapter of the Technological Institute of Morelia, a group of students passionate about AI and machine learning.

Objective

Development and research in the field of artificial intelligence, as well as dissemination and knowledge sharing within the ITM community and the general public.

Areas of Interest

  • Internet of Things
  • Data Science
  • Natural Language Processing
  • Computer Vision
  • Large Language Models
  • Intelligent Agents
  • Autonomous Vehicles and Drones
